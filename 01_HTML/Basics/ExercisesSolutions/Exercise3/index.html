How To Make Perfect Cheesecake

What you need

Ingredients

For the cheesecake

  • Butter for coating the pan
  • 2 pounds full-flat cream cheese
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch, or 2 tablespoon all-pourpose flour (optional)
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons freshly squeezed lemon juie (optional)
  • 2 traspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 large egg yolk

For the crust

  • 12 whole graham cracker rentangles (6 ounces)
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter

Equipment

  • 9-inch or 10-inch springform pan
  • Aluminum foil
  • Food processor
  • Stand mixer or electric hand mixer
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Spatula
  • Roasting pan or other dish big enough to hold the springform pan

Instructions

  1. Prehet the oven and warm the cream cheese. Arrange a rack in the lower-middle position of the oven and heat to 350F. Take the blocks of cream cheese out their boxex and let them come to room temperature on the counter whule tou prepare the curst, about 30 minutes
  2. Coat the pan with butter. Use your fingers to coat a small pat of butter all over the bottom and sides of a 9-inch or 10-inch pan.
  3. Wrap the pan in foil. Cut 2 large piecess of foil and place them on your work surface on top of each other in a cross. Set the springform pan in the middle and fold the edges of the foil up around the sides od the pan. The foil gives you extra protection against water getting into the pan during the water waher bath step.
